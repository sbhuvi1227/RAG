COLLEGE ACADEMIC AND PLACEMENT INFORMATION

1. Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
The CSE department offers B.Tech and M.Tech programs.
Key subjects include Data Structures, Algorithms, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Database Systems, Operating Systems, and Cloud Computing.
The department has specialized labs for AI Research, Data Analytics, and Cybersecurity.
The head of department is Dr. R. Kumar.

2. Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
The ECE department offers B.Tech and M.Tech programs.
Core subjects include Digital Signal Processing, Embedded Systems, VLSI Design, and Wireless Communication.
The department operates a VLSI Design Lab and Communication Systems Lab.
The head of department is Dr. Meena S.

3. Department of Mechanical Engineering
Offers B.Tech program.
Specializations include Thermal Engineering, Robotics, Manufacturing Systems.
The Robotics Lab supports industrial automation projects.

4. Faculty Information
